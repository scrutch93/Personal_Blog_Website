Mario Movie?!!


For all of those that either grew up spending hours clearing level after level of stomping goombas and koopas, there is awesome news. Nintendo has partnered with Illumination to produce a Super Mario Bros movie! The feature film will star Chris Pratt as Mario and as Luigi. This for sure will be a treat of nostalgia for fans of all ages.
